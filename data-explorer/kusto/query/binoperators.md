Kusto supports a number of bitwise (binary) operators between integers:

* [binary-not](binary-notfunction.md)
* [binary-or](binary-orfunction.md)
* [binary-shift-left](binary-shift-leftfunction.md)
* [binary-shift-right](binary-shift-rightfunction.md)
